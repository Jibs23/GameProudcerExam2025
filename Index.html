Become a game producer

Who is it for?

On the Game Producer Program, we give you the practical skills and tools required to lead small to medium-sized productions.

This program is specifically tailored to:

  • Future producers and leads
  • Producers and leads in the established game industry
  • CEOs in smaller companies
  • Entrepreneurs and solo developers in the game industry


Whether you are currently employed in the industry or trying to become a part of it, we have put together a program that will fit your needs. We will upgrade your skills, prepare you for the meeting with the game industry, expand upon your knowledge base and dive deeper into certain aspects of the producer role.

If you are starting a new business or climbing the career ladder, we will help you establish a solid foundation and broaden your network to make your business flourish. So if you have the ambition to lead and are looking for a "full stack" approach to producing, or just curious to dive deeper into a narrow aspect of it, our education is the right choice for you.

The Program

The Game Producer Program consists of three modules that run over the period of 10 months:


Project Management (Q3 & Q4 2025 - 10 sessions)
  • Differentiate between various phases in production
  • Facilitating production in small to mid-sized teams
  • Adapting production flow to your team and the deliverables

Product Management (Q1 2026 - 10 sessions)
  • Understanding the product from an end-user’s as well as the markets perspective
  • Monetization, User Acquisition, business cases and –plans
  • Pitching your game to investors and publishers

Leadership (Q2 2026 - 10 sessions)
  • Motivating your direct reports
  • Giving productive feedback
  • The leadership discipline in the creative business

30 sessions of 2 hours spread across 9 months

All sessions are available to join online

Each participant is offered up to 2 one-on-one coaching sessions per module (1 Hour)

3 assesments (one for each module)
